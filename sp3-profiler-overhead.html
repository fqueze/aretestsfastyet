Speedometer 3 Profiler Overhead

Loads each browsertime-benchmark-firefox-speedometer3 task's profile_resource-usage.json artifact and reads the per-iteration Speedometer 3 scores the test itself prints (one "score": { … "mean": <N> } JSON block per iteration, where the mean field is the iteration's runs/min score). The profiled iteration is the one whose score block is emitted after profiler.start. Time overhead is reported as baseline_mean_score / profiled_score − 1 — i.e. how much longer the profiled iteration took per Speedometer run, relative to the mean of the other iterations. Mochitest SP3 jobs are intentionally skipped since they don't produce a profiled iteration. Click any row or chart bar to open the task's resource-usage profile in profiler.firefox.com; alt-click opens the TaskCluster Tools view instead.
